Membership

Membership Rewards

Membership in the Illuminati Lodge of New York opens doors to experiences, knowledge, and connections that transcend ordinary life. Our members enjoy a range of benefits designed to support their personal, professional, and spiritual growth.

Access to Sacred Knowledge

Unlock centuries of wisdom through our extensive library of texts, teachings, and oral traditions passed down through generations of enlightened minds.

Global Network

Connect with over 50,000 members across 120+ countries. Our network includes leaders in business, science, arts, and government.

Leadership Opportunities

Rise through the degrees of our order and take on leadership roles that allow you to shape the future of our brotherhood.

International Events

Attend exclusive gatherings, ceremonies, and events at our lodges around the world. Experience the unity of our global brotherhood firsthand.

Philanthropic Impact

Participate in charitable initiatives that create real change. Our collective contributions exceed $1 billion annually.

Mutual Support

Benefit from the support of brothers in times of need. Our network provides assistance in personal, professional, and spiritual matters.

Personal Growth

Access mentorship programs, workshops, and resources designed to help you reach your highest potential.

Spiritual Development

Engage in practices and ceremonies that promote spiritual enlightenment and self-discovery.

Begin Your Journey

The rewards of membership extend far beyond material benefits. Join a brotherhood that has guided seekers for over two centuries.
